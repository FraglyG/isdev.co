Alfred User Guide

Alfred Set Up

Introduce yourself using the Alfred FYI command.

alfred fyi

Alfred uses your introduction to remember who you are, including your gender and more, allowing for tailored interactions that suit your preferences.

Example: alfred fyi I'm Jimmy, a 19 year old male swedish student. I study agriculture at harvard.


Set your timezone with the Alfred timezone command.

alfred timezone

This will ensure alfred takes your local time into account when talking to you, allowing for more accurate and specific responses to queries.

How to Talk to Alfred

Start a conversation

You can start a conversation by saying "Hey Alfred", this is just one of many ways to initiate a conversation. You can also immediately append your query after "Hey Alfred".
Example: "Hey alfred, how are you?"

End a conversation

You can end a conversation by saying "That's all" / "That is all". This is, again, just one of many ways to end a conversation.

FAQ

It takes a few minutes for the Patreon bot to update your role. If you haven't received your role within 15 minutes, you can ping Fragly for help after joining the server.

You can use the 'alfred token usage' command to track your current token usage.

Alfred doesn't store past conversations, although I have considered adding persistent memory in the future, allowing Alfred to remember specific things that has happened in past conversations.
