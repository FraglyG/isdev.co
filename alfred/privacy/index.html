Privacy Policy

At Alfred, we take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using Alfred, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

What information does Alfred collect?

Alfred only collects and stores Discord user IDs and occasionally usernames in order to provide its services. We do not collect any other personal information, such as email addresses or IP addresses, unless the user provided that information to alfred themselves.

How does Alfred use this information?

Alfred only uses the information it collects to provide its services to you, such as responding to your commands in Discord. We do not share this information with any third parties, except as required by law.

How does Alfred protect this information?

Alfred takes reasonable measures to protect the information it collects from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use industry-standard security measures, such as encryption and access controls, to protect your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes to the policy, we will notify changes in the discord server.
